SHORT-TERM RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Short-Term Rental Agreement is executed on June 10, 2025, between Harbor View Rentals Pty Ltd ("Owner") and Emma Thompson ("Guest").

1. PREMISES
The furnished studio apartment at 12 Bayside Lane, Sydney NSW 2000, Australia.

2. RENTAL PERIOD
This rental is for a fixed term beginning July 1, 2025 and ending September 30, 2025. This Agreement terminates automatically on the end date with no further action required by either party. This Agreement does NOT auto-renew under any circumstances.

3. RENT
Guest shall pay rent of AUD 3,200 per month, payable in advance on the first day of each month of the rental period.

4. DEPOSIT
A refundable bond of AUD 3,200 is payable upon signing.

5. NOTICE
If either party wishes to terminate prior to the end date, fourteen (14) days written notice is required.

6. TERMINATION
The Owner may terminate this Agreement immediately for (a) damage to the Premises beyond normal wear, or (b) failure to pay rent when due.

7. NO ESCALATION
Rent is fixed for the term and shall not increase.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia.

Signed:
Owner: Harbor View Rentals Pty Ltd
Guest: Emma Thompson
