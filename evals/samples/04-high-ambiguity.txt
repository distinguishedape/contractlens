LEASE ARRANGEMENT

This lease arrangement is made this day of October 2024 between the Owner (hereinafter the "First Party") and the Occupant (hereinafter the "Second Party"), for the property situated in the general area of Mumbai.

The First Party permits the Second Party to use the premises for a reasonable period, commencing on or about the first of November, 2024, and continuing for such time as the parties may mutually determine appropriate.

Rent shall be a reasonable amount, understood between the parties to be approximately Rs. 35,000, give or take, payable monthly or as otherwise agreed.

A deposit in a customary sum shall be held by the First Party.

Rent may be revisited periodically and adjusted as deemed fit by the First Party, taking into account market conditions.

Either party may end this arrangement upon reasonable notice. What constitutes reasonable notice shall be determined in good faith.

The arrangement may continue beyond the initial period unless one party clearly indicates otherwise. Notice of such indication should ideally be provided in advance.

This arrangement is subject to the applicable laws of India.
