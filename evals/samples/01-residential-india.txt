RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on January 15, 2025, by and between Sunrise Properties LLC ("Landlord") and Priya Sharma ("Tenant"), with Rakesh Sharma acting as Guarantor.

1. PREMISES
The Landlord leases to Tenant the apartment located at 42 MG Road, Bangalore, Karnataka 560001.

2. TERM
The term of this lease shall commence on February 1, 2025, and end on January 31, 2026.

3. RENT
Tenant agrees to pay monthly rent of INR 45,000 (Indian Rupees Forty-Five Thousand) on or before the 5th day of each calendar month.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of INR 90,000 as a security deposit, refundable at the end of the term less any lawful deductions.

5. RENT ESCALATION
The monthly rent shall increase by 8 percent at the time of each renewal term.

6. NOTICE PERIOD
Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving 60 days written notice to the other party.

7. TERMINATION CONDITIONS
The Landlord may terminate this Agreement immediately upon (a) non-payment of rent for more than 15 days, (b) material breach of any covenant herein, or (c) use of the Premises for any unlawful purpose.

8. AUTO-RENEWAL
Unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days prior to the expiration date, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms on the same terms and conditions, except that the monthly rent shall increase as provided in Section 5.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Karnataka, India.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.

Landlord: Sunrise Properties LLC
Tenant: Priya Sharma
Guarantor: Rakesh Sharma
